Affiliate Marketing Made Simple

Affiliate marketing is one of the easiest ways to make money online. You don’t need your own product, customer support, or inventory. All you do is recommend products and earn a commission when people buy through your link.

  1. Sign up: Join affiliate programs like Amazon Associates, ClickBank, or Digistore24.
  2. Create Content: Write blogs, make YouTube videos, or share posts on social media with your affiliate links.
  3. Get Traffic: Use SEO, Pinterest, or TikTok to bring visitors to your content.
  4. Earn in USD: Every sale through your link gives you a commission, usually between 5% and 70% depending on the product.

✅ The key is consistency. The more content you publish, the more chances you have to earn while you sleep.
