How to Make $100/Day with AI Tools

Artificial Intelligence is changing how people make money online. The best part? Many of these tools are free or low-cost, and anyone can start using them today.

  1. Pick a Niche: Choose a topic people are always interested in, like fitness, money, or education.
  2. Use AI Tools: Free AI tools like ChatGPT, Jasper (paid), or Writesonic can help you create blog posts, social media captions, and even YouTube scripts.
  3. Add Affiliate Links: Sign up for platforms like ClickBank or Amazon Associates.
  4. Share Automatically: Use free tools like Buffer or Later to schedule posts on social media.

✅ If you stay consistent, it’s realistic to reach $100/day within months by combining blog traffic, social media, and affiliate marketing.
